FASHION PRODUCT RECOMMENDEDATIONS

About Project

This project developed an advanced convolutional neural network (CNN) model for an e-commerce fashion product recommendation system. Trained on 15,000 diverse images, the model uses ResNet50 for detailed feature extraction and k-nearest neighbors (KNN) for identifying similar products. ResNet50 captures intricate image patterns, while KNN finds the five most similar items based on feature proximity. This model enhances e-commerce platforms by providing personalized product recommendations, helping customers quickly find preferred items and increasing satisfaction. It creates cross-selling opportunities, boosts sales, and aids in inventory management by identifying popular trends. Personalized recommendations also drive marketing engagement and conversion rates, contributing to a more dynamic, customer-centric shopping experience.
